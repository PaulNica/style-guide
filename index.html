Text styles

  • font-family: Montserrat
  • font-weight: 600 (Bold)
  • font-size: 36px

H2: Subheading, used for all section as a main title standard

  • font-family: Roboto
  • font-weight: 500 (Medium)
  • font-size: 25px

P: Paragraph text, used for every other content on the page

  • font-family: Nunito Sans
  • font-weight: 300 (Light)
  • font-size: 16px
